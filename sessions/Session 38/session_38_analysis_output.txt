They successfully navigated the final stretch of their journey, defeated a powerful warlord and his monstrous secret weapon, solved the puzzle of the fake tree, and successfully delivered the eggs, fully accomplishing their mission.

    *   **Stage Title: Rising Action - The Final Approach and the Court of Chaos.**

        *   **Key Events within Stage:** Discovering the portal within the painting, entering the idyllic grove, placing the eggs in the Tree of Life, and Gooch making his final, absurd wish.
