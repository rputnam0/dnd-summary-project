## Key NPCs

*   **Captain Levi:** Stonewall Legionnaire officer. Wears distinctive 'bucket' hat and vest, wields advanced hand crossbows. Oversees Legionnaire tryouts. Identified the PCs' 'Isoptic Curse' potential, recruited them, revealing he and other powerful soldiers are also cursed. Directed the party to Hamhock. Casual but authoritative demeanor. Observed by Zariel in a meeting discussing another domed city. Personally assigned the party's first mission (Case 22391).
*   **Lord Marshall Thurr:** High-ranking Stonewall Legionnaire leader. Described as a 'bear of a man' with a full beard, scar over one eye, and cob pipe. Observed the tryouts from a balcony. Confirmed the Legionnaires were screening for individuals with the Isoptic Curse. Endorsed Levi's offer to the PCs. Jumped into the fight against the Cursed Fire Elemental, demonstrating combat prowess and command presence. Initiated the cursed soldier program.
*   **Hamhawk:** Ogre-like (6'9", 500 lbs) female humanoid smithy for the Stonewall Legionnaires. Gruff, efficient, protective of her advanced forge in the Storm District. Has an underbite, sharpened canines, tiny pince-nez glasses, and clean blonde hair in a pigtail. Crafted the party's initial custom Legionnaire gear. Expressed interest in Zariel being a nature caster. Provided Leopold with schematics for his gear.
*   **Donnie Farsight (aka Ratshit):** Malnourished-looking (~5'11", 80 lbs) half-orc handler assigned to the party. Cynical, fast-talking, prefers the nickname "Ratshit" (earned after losing a fight to a giant rat). Explained barracks procedures, gear functions (healing, comms, vitals monitoring), mission assignments, and the pay/lottery system. Delivered the party's gear and first mission folder.
*   **Selena Sal:** Eccentric, incredibly old woman running a cluttered potion shop in the Storm District. Wears huge goggles, a messy bun, a patchwork muumuu, and flip-flops. Seems potentially senile or feigning it. Engaged the party in bizarre conversation, introducing her talking hermit crab companion, Marcel. Her shop contains the hidden entrance to the Fungal Tunnels. Her involvement/awareness is unknown.
*   **Unnamed Posh Nobleman:** Arrogant, well-dressed participant in the Legionnaire tryouts; Kaladin's opponent. Wielded a rapier. Objected vehemently after being defeated, revealed he was also cursed, lost control, and transformed into a large Fire Elemental. Defeated by the party and Thurr. *(Status: Deceased/Destroyed as Fire Elemental)*

## Important Events So Far

*   **Induction into the Stonewall Legionnaires:** Kaladin, Leopold, and Zariel individually attended Legionnaire tryouts in New New Arbor. During the trials, they unexpectedly revealed potent, innate magical abilities tied to the 'Isoptic Curse', attracting the specific interest of Captain Levi and Lord Marshall Thurr. After surviving their individual trials and collectively defeating a cursed nobleman who transformed into a violent Fire Elemental, the trio accepted formal offers to join the Stonewall Legionnaires, embarking on a new, uncertain path.

## Locations of Interest

*   **Avarias:** The campaign world/region.
*   **New New Arbor:** A large, technologically advanced city contained within a dome. Light filters through the dome's opaque ceiling. Divided into distinct districts with varying functions and social strata; travel between districts requires permission or Legionnaire status. Features advanced arcane technology integrated into infrastructure.
    *   **Market District:** Practical, dense, possibly lower socioeconomic status ("slums" mentioned).
    *   **Dawn District:** Affluent, sleek architecture. Site of reported undead activity (per Zariel's observation).
    *   **Sweets District:** Moderate housing, features lawns.
    *   **Amberfields:** Sprawling farmland within the dome.
    *   **Emerald Grove:** Forested district, home to nature magic users, less dense. Zariel found his orchid nearby.
    *   **Storm District:** Perpetually overcast and rainy district with Dwarven architectural influences, known for business, armories, and manufacturing. Location of Hamhawk's Forge, Selena Sal's Potion Shop, the underground bar, and the entrance to the Fungal Tunnels. Site of recent disappearances and Myconid activity.
    *   **The Core:** Central hub district. Contains Legionnaire HQ/Training Grounds and Barracks. Where resources are distributed. Arcane energy pipes run beneath it in the underpassage.
*   **Underpassage Tunnel System (Beneath Core District):** Dark maintenance tunnels carrying arcane energy via glowing/thrumming pipes. Prone to steam bursts creating haze. Zariel's former workplace and Leopold's initial location after reactivation.
*   **Stonewall Legionnaire Training Grounds / Combat Arena (Core District):** Large, warehouse-like building with vaulted ceilings. Site of the Legionnaire tryouts. Features registration areas, testing stations, a central sand-filled combat pit, and raised viewing balconies for officials.
*   **Stonewall Legionnaire Barracks (Core District):** Enormous, hangar-like structure serving as living quarters and operational hub. Contains bunk areas, private rooms, mission rooms, listing boards, mess hall, and arcane infrastructure. Party's current residence.
*   **Hamhawk's Forge (Storm District):** Large, metal building housing Hamhawk's highly advanced smithy. Filled with tools, prototypes, magical components, and advanced machinery (arcane etch guns, gravity spell press). Site where the party commissioned their gear.
*   **Selena Sal's Potion Shop (Storm District, SE):** Small, cluttered, dusty wooden shack appearing as a potion shop run by the eccentric Selena Sal. Contains the hidden entrance (beneath floorboards in the storeroom) to the Fungal Tunnels.
*   **Underground Bar (Storm District):** Subterranean rave-like bar with loud, alternative music (Norse/fiddle house), flashing lights, neon drinks, and an eclectic clientele. Leopold was briefly dragged inside.
*   **Fungal Tunnels (Beneath Selena's Shop, Storm District):** A network of initially narrow, dark earthen tunnels opening into wider passages coated in moist fungal networks. Lit by ambient blue bioluminescence from plants and ground fog. Smells of decay and fertilizer. Inhabited by Myconid Sprouts and at least one Sovereign. Site of the party's first mission combat. Extent unknown.
