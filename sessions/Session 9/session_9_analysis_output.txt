**13. Battle, Puzzle, and Challenge Breakdown:**

*   **Challenge: Infiltration and Stealth**
    *   **Nature of the Challenge:** Silently entering the Court of Viteo mausoleum and navigating the initial tunnels without alerting cultists.
    *   **Party Approach and Tactics:** Used Zariel's Pass Without Trace, careful movement, perception checks, and Leopold's trap detection.
    *   **Successes and Failures:** Successfully infiltrated undetected.

*   **Battle: Father Kallor / The Reverend and Minions**
    *   **Nature of the Challenge:**
        *   Phase 1: Father Kallor and two Monk Cultists. Kallor as a spellcaster, monks as melee.
        *   Phase 2: The Reverend (transformed Kallor - powerful angelic being with a greatsword, radiant attacks, flight, Lair Actions, Legendary Actions) and two reanimated Undead Soldiers.
    *   **Party Approach and Tactics:**
        *   Phase 1: Leopold quickly neutralized monks. Kaladin and Leopold focused fire on Kallor. Zariel moved to engage. Ratchet provided Bless.
        *   Phase 2: Zariel (as bear) engaged The Reverend directly. Kaladin used Fire Bolt then Thunderwave for control. Leopold used Ray of Frost and Magic Stones, trying to maintain distance. Ratchet used Shatter and Toll the Dead. Party focused fire where possible, reacting to The Reverend's high damage output and minion summoning.
    *   **Successes and Failures:**
        *   Successes: Monks neutralized quickly. Consistent damage on Kallor/Reverend. Kaladin's Thunderwave effectively controlled undead. Ratchet maintained Bless. Leopold landed a crit on The Reverend.
        *   Failures: Zariel took massive damage and was downed. Ratchet was heavily wounded. The party struggled against The Reverend's high damage and regenerative Lair Action. Initial ranged attacks from Kaladin/Leopold missed Kallor.
    *   **Consequences:** Battle is ongoing and desperate. Zariel is unconscious and making death saves. The party is aware of massive external threats (city uprising, Core attack) complicating their focus. Zariel's Warforged nature partially revealed.
