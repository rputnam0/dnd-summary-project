**7. Rules and Game Mechanics Impact:**

It nullified spells like `Haste` and `Moonbeam`, disabled magic items (`Ring of Protection`, etc.), and forced the party to either use non-magical attacks or physically move out of the effect, which they did by jumping into the chasm.

This created constant, unpredictable pressure. The variety of rays (Paralyzing, Petrification, Disintegration-Force, Telekinesis, Slowing) tested the entire party's saving throws.
*   **Condition Immunities:** Zariel's immunity to Petrification as a Fire Elemental (01:26:56) and Cyrus's immunity to being slowed from `Freedom of Movement` (02:16:11) were crucial saves that prevented devastating debuffs.
*   **Smites and Flourishes:** Cyrus's Bard/Paladin abilities (`Divine Smite`, Defensive Flourish) were the party's main source of burst damage, allowing him to bypass the Queen's high HP pool and deliver massive hits.
*   **Polymorph:** Zariel's use of `Polymorph` on Kaladen was a core strategy, transforming the wizard into a high-HP, high-damage melee brute, effectively giving the party an extra front-line fighter.
