**6. Important Locations and Objects:**

*   **The Wellspring Chamber:**
    *   **Detailed Description:** A large, dark stone room at the core of Moonhaven. It houses an enormous stone well with faded runes. Before being disabled, a powerful arcane trap hovered over the well, appearing as a flat, golden, runic circle that violently siphoned a massive torrent of water into a portal to the Astral Sea. The room is littered with ancient, moss-covered bones and a dilapidated control console. After the trap is disabled, the well begins to weakly emit wisps of blue magical energy.
    *   **Function and Purpose:** The source of magical power for the North Dome. It was subverted by an ancient trap to banish a great enemy, with the unintended side effect of flooding the city for centuries.
    *   **Narrative Significance:** This is a key location for the campaign. Finding and restoring it was a primary mission objective and a crucial step in Kaladen's personal quest. The recording found here provided vital lore about the Cursed War and the fall of Moonhaven.

*   **The Marble Eye:**
    *   **Detailed Description:** A grapefruit-sized, marble-black eyeball, the last remnant of the Spider Queen.
    *   **Function and Purpose:** Identified as a legendary wondrous item requiring attunement. It grants +2 AC, advantage on saves against all spells, and the ability to cast *Counterspell* or *Dispel Magic* at 3rd level once per day.     *   **Narrative Significance:** This is a massive power boost for Cyrus and the party. It transforms him from a primarily melee skirmisher into a formidable defensive powerhouse. The item itself is a direct link to a defeated Cursed General.

*   **The Embered Mane:**
    *   **Detailed Description:** A magical half-cloak of brilliant flaming feathers. It can expand into a full cape when its power is activated.
    *   **Function and Purpose:** A legendary relic that grants fire resistance and allows the wearer to transform into a Fire Elemental by expending both Wild Shape uses.

    *   **Detailed Description:** A black, empty abyss of open space, filled with distant, glittering lights of energy. A portal etched into nothingness serves as the entry/exit point, with the siphoned water swirling out into the void like a galaxy.
