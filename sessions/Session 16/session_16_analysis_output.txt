    *   **Attribution:** DM.
    *   **Reason (If Stated):** Complexity of tracking multiple effects in a long combat.
    *   **Narrative Impact:** Potentially made the fight slightly easier or harder depending on the specific instance, but overall the party focused on consistent silver application.
    *   **Type of Adjustment:** Inconsistent Mechanic Application rather than a true retcon.
    *   **Timeline Integration:** The timeline notes silvered hits. The exact healing amounts prevented are hard to track precisely due to this inconsistency. The important factor is that the party *believed* consistent silvering was crucial and acted accordingly.

**7. Rules and Game Mechanics Impact:**

*   **Lycanthrope Damage Immunity/Regeneration & Silvered Weapons:** This was the central mechanic of the encounter. The Alpha's immunity to non-silvered/non-magical BPS and its regeneration necessitated the party's focus on silvered attacks. The successful application of silver was key to their victory.
*   **Pass Without Trace:** Zariel's spell was crucial for the party's stealthy approach to the den, allowing them to achieve surprise by collapsing the entrance.
*   **Conjure Animals (Zariel):** Summoning 8 wolves drastically changed the action economy, providing multiple targets for the Alpha, dealing chip damage, and triggering Pack Tactics for themselves. Though eventually defeated, they bought valuable time and absorbed attacks.
*   **Frightful Presence (Alpha Werewolf):** While the PCs were immune due to the 24-hour rule from the previous encounter, it significantly impacted Zariel's summoned wolves, causing many to be frightened and less effective.
*   **Legendary Actions (Alpha Werewolf):** Allowed the Alpha to maintain offensive pressure even when outnumbered, contributing to the fight's difficulty and length.
*   **Saving Throws (various):** Crucial for resisting effects like the Alpha's bite infection (Kaladen failed), Frightful Presence (wolves), and spell effects (*Create Bonfire*, *Thunderwave*). Leopold's successful *Shield* spell saves were also critical.
*   **Critical Hits/Fails:** Kaladen's critical hit with Light Splitter dealt significant damage. Zariel's critical fail on a save vs the Alpha's bite (as a bear) led to him dropping form. The Alpha's Nat 1 on a bite against Zariel was a moment of comic relief/tension break.
*   **Action Surge (Kaladen):** Allowed Kaladen a burst of attacks at a crucial moment.
*   **Arcana Check (Kaladen):** The DC 22 Arcana check for successfully amputating his arm without further complication was a high-stakes roll with significant narrative consequences.
*   **Potion of Soulwood Prosthesis:** The mechanics of this magic item provided a direct path to resolving Kaladen's amputation in a fantastical way.
